Who We Are

Happy Paws Pet Adoption is a local animal rescue group that is dedicated to giving homeless cats and dogs a second chance at life with their unique, compassionate style. Collaborating closely with dedicated volunteers, foster carers and veterinary clinics we ensure that animals in need are safe, receive medical care and emotional support. Our vision is to save, rehabilitate and place pets in a caring home with a caring family where they can flourish. We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and engaging with our community to make our world a kinder place where every animal can be safe, loved and cared for.


Our Mission

Our goal is to save, rehabilitate and rehome animals in need, giving them a second chance to live happy and healthy lives. We also strive to educate the community about the need to adopt pets and responsible pet ownership, and how to treat pets with love, respect and commitment.


Why Pet Adoption Matters

People adopt pets every day because thousands of animals are abandoned and given away to shelters – for the most part without anyone's fault. Adopting animals in the shelter system helps defuse animal populations, provides them a second chance for a safe, loving home and helps reduce shelter crowding. Not only does it save lives but it also provides happiness, companionship and emotional support to pets and new owners.
